  1. Fintech · 2024

    Nexus — Banking Redesign

    A complete overhaul of a neobank's mobile experience for 2.3M active users, refocused on financial clarity over feature density.

    The Challenge

    Nexus had accrued 6 years of feature debt. Customer satisfaction scores had fallen 18 points; users were abandoning complex tasks mid-flow. The brief: redesign without losing power users.

    My Approach

    I led a 10-week discovery sprint — contextual inquiry with 24 users, diary studies over 2 weeks, and a competitive audit of 11 apps. The insight: users wanted a trusted advisor, not a transaction engine.

    Outcome

    Task completion time
    −34%
    CSAT score
    +22 pts
    Support tickets
    −41%

    • Interaction Design
    • Design System
    • Prototyping
  2. Healthcare · 2023

    MedFlow — Clinical Dashboard

    A clinical workflow tool built from the ground up with accessibility as architecture, not afterthought.

    The Challenge

    Clinicians using legacy EMR software were spending 40% of their shift navigating software. Errors caused by UI friction had real patient-safety implications.

    My Approach

    Embedded for 3 weeks in two ICUs, shadowing nurses and physicians. Ran structured usability tests with clinicians of varying technical literacy. Prioritised keyboard navigability and high-contrast modes from day one.

    Outcome

    Hospitals deployed
    47
    Training required
    Zero
    Accessibility
    WCAG 2.1 AA
    • Accessibility
    • Data Visualisation
    • Usability Testing
    • Information Architecture
  3. SaaS · 2022

    Canvas — Design System

    A multi-brand token-based design system unifying 12 product teams under one coherent visual language.

    The Challenge

    After three acquisitions, the company had four codebases, six Figma libraries, and zero design coherence. The goal: one system, many brands.

    My Approach

    Ran a 3-month audit of all existing components. Established a tier-1/tier-2/tier-3 token architecture so brands could override visual personality without touching core behaviour. Wrote the governance model and contribution guide.

    Outcome

    Teams adopted
    12
    Brands supported
    3
    Component velocity
    +3×
    • Design Systems
    • Component Library
    • Documentation
    • Stakeholder Management
