Biography

I'm Alex Rivera — a designer who started in print and ended up building products used by millions.

After studying graphic design at the California College of the Arts, I spent three years as a front-end developer at an agency. That detour turned out to be formative: I learned to read code reviews, sit in sprint planning, and understand why designs get renegotiated at the implementation stage.

Since pivoting to product design I've worked at early-stage startups, a Series B health-tech company, and a publicly traded fintech. Right now I'm open to senior IC or lead roles where I can own a problem space from discovery through delivery.

Outside work: I restore vintage mechanical keyboards, run slowly, and grow tomatoes with inconsistent results.
